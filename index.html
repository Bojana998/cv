Bojana Rakic

Student at the Faculty of Applied Arts

Bojana Rakic is a final year students at the Faculty of Applied Arts in Belgrade, Serbia. Her fields of interest are printmaking, illustration, animation and graphic design. She participated in many exhibitions while on faculty among which are Small Print Form Exhibition 2019 and May Exhibition of Printmaking 2020. She won a second place for poster design for International Children's Theatre Festival in Subotica, Serbia. She is currently working as freelance graphic designer and illustrator.
Her digital illustrations are mostly combinations of vivid colors and bold brush strokes but she is trying to experiment as much as possible in order to find her own style. She likes creating something fun and surreal.
